Chicken Teriyaki

  • Time: 2 hrs
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
White rice 3 cups
Chicken 4 chicken breasts
No ketchup,
just teriyaki sauce		 1 cup

Step 1: Steam the rice
Step 2: Bake the chicken for 1 hour
Step 3:Put teriyaki sauce on top of the chicken

Salad

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Lettuce 2 bunches
Chicken 2 breasts
Balsalmic vinagrette 1/4 cup
Carrots 2 carrots

Step 1: Cut up the letuce
Step 2: Shred the carrots
Step 3: Pan fry the chicken until brown
Step 4: Slice the chicken
Step 5: Mix all of the ingredients together with the balsalmic vinagrette

Hamburgers

  • Time: 1 hr
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Ground beef 2 pounds
Lettuce 1 bunch
Burger buns 4 buns
Tomatoes 2 tomatoes
Onions 2 onions

Step 1: Shape the beef into 4 patties
Step 2: Cook the patties until they are brown
Step 3: Slice the onions and tomatoes
Step 4: Put all the ingredients on the bun
